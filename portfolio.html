Anagram Oracle

October 2025

The Anagram Oracle was an idea I concieved in mid 2025. It would consist of an immense, clock-like mechanism, into which a spectator could enter their name. The Oracle would then rearrange the name's letters to provide a prophecy/statement regarding that person's fate. This was a partial proof-of-concept, which reverses each word in a short poem to create an alternative poem.

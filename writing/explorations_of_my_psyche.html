I often have difficulty making friends, for various reasons. Consider these as stages:
1) Similar interests: My interests are often very esoteric and scattered, and if my friends' interests do not have a sufficient overlap I tend to distance myself.
2) Once you have become someone who I cherish, I want to build palaces for you. I want to craft elaborate labyrinths and adventures for you to embark on. It galls me to give trivial gifts.
